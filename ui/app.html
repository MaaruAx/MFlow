MFlow Quick Reference
01 Connect MFlow auto-connects to an open DaVinci Resolve session on launch.
02 Scan Click Scan All or press CtrlR to detect animated nodes.
03 Select Check the inputs you want to affect in the scan panel.
04 Shape Drag handles in the curve editor, or pick a preset.
05 Apply Press Apply to write the curve to the selected keyframes.

The Panels menu lets you hide or tear off panels into separate windows.

Auto Apply writes the curve on every handle drag, without pressing Apply.
